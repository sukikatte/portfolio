About Me

The Designer's Mindset

I'm a software engineer with a passion for creating technology that truly resonates with people. My journey began with functional programming, but evolved into a deep exploration of how systems can be designed to serve human needs.

My Design Philosophy

功能为基、体验为魂 — Functionality is the foundation, experience is the soul. I believe that powerful technology becomes meaningful only when it connects authentically with users. Every line of code, every interaction, every visual element should serve the ultimate goal: creating systems that people find intuitive, enjoyable, and empowering.

"Years of playing music taught me how timing shapes emotion — a principle I now apply to designing intuitive user flows. Drawing trained my eye for visual balance, which guides my interface design decisions."

Beyond Code

My interests in music and visual arts aren't separate from my technical work — they inform it. Music teaches rhythm and emotional pacing. Art teaches composition and visual hierarchy. These disciplines come together in every project I create, helping me build experiences that feel both technically sound and humanly resonant.

What Drives Me

I'm particularly drawn to projects that bridge multiple domains: technology and education, systems and creativity, functionality and delight. Whether building a wildlife conservation platform or a real-time multiplayer game, I focus on creating experiences that are innovative, user-centered, and meaningful.

Let's build technology that truly connects with people.
